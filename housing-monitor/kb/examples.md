# Examples: Relevant vs Irrelevant

## Relevant
**Title**: "Ordinance Amending Municipal Code to Strengthen Tenant Anti-Harassment Protections"
**Reason**: Directly affects landlord behavior and tenant rights.

**Title**: "Resolution Establishing a Rental Registry Fee of $100 per Unit"
**Reason**: Imposes a direct financial obligation on landlords.

**Title**: "Discussion of AB 1482 Implementation and Local Just Cause Add-ons"
**Reason**: Relates to state rent control and potential local expansion.

## Irrelevant
**Title**: "Approval of Minutes for the Regular Meeting of June 15"
**Reason**: Administrative procedure.

**Title**: "Proclamation Declaring July as 'Parks and Recreation Month'"
**Reason**: Ceremonial, no legislative impact.

**Title**: "Contract Award for Office Supplies for City Hall"
**Reason**: Operational expense, unrelated to housing policy.

**Title**: "Zoning Variance for 123 Main St to allow a 2-foot setback encroachment"
**Reason**: Specific to one property, not a policy change.
